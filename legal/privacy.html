Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (email, payment details via Stripe, project URLs) and automatically (page views, IP addresses, device type). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process payments, send service-related communications, and generate anonymized analytics. We will never use your data for purposes unrelated to the services you requested.

3. PIPEDA Compliance

As a Canadian-operated service, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information. Requests can be sent to our privacy contact below.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Audit logs through Clawforce are retained for a minimum of 7 years as required by compliance standards.

We use Stripe for payment processing, GitHub for hosting, and may use analytics services such as PostHog. Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and optional analytics cookies. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings without affecting core functionality.
